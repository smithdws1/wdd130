Rafting Site Plan

David Smith

Overview

Purpose

I like to whitewater raft. It's exhilirating! I want to document my rafting adventures and provide a guide business for others to learn to enjoy whitewater rafting as well. I'll rate different rivers and expeditions to make it easier for others to find one they'd enjoy!

Audience

The audience is people looking for outdoor adventures. Targeting those within the 35-65 age range with teenage kids, the 18-35 year old, singles who want to enjoy some time outdoors, and the mid 40's couples who have hiked everything else and now want to try something new.
